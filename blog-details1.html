2019 AUDI

The four rings of Audi rides on a wide assortment of 2019 products assembled with a mix of modern premium comfort and unique powertrains. While quattro all-wheel drive remains a prominent centerpiece for the German auto brand, the 2019 model year is highlighted with the introduction of an all-electric e-tron crossover.

Another new addition to Audi's lineup is the Q8 featuring a large five-passenger cabin paired with exciting exterior looks. The two brand-new crossovers are joined by a redesigned Audi Q3. An all-new Audi A8 sedan, Sportback version of the 444-horsepower RS 5 and an updated A7 Sportback are also new additions to the 2019 model year.

While the Audi R8 will not be available for the 2019 model year, the Audi product line's performance seekers may be enticed by the four-doors of the new S8 propelled by a 563-horsepower TFSI V-8 engine.
